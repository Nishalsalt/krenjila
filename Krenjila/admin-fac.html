ACADEMICS AND FACILITIES


Triyog provides excellent child-centered education for all-round development through its distinctive approach of learning by doing. Our pupils learn in an environment ideal and conducive for self-learning where teachers assume the role of facilitators. We focus on our students' needs, interests, aptitude,and abilities so that they become reflective individuals who love learning. We expect the close participation and cooperation of parents alongside their need for patience to help each young scholar bloom.



Levels


At Triyog High School, the academic block is divided into four levels:
Primary (Kindergarten to Grade III)

Junior (Grades IV & V)

Middle (Grades VI to VIII)

High (Grades IX to XII)
